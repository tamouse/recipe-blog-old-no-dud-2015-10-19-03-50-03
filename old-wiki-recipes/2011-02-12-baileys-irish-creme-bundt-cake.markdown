This is a hugely delicious and greatly enjoyed cake. People go back
for two or three slices.

!! Cake
!!!Ingredients

* 1 pkg Yellow cake mix
* 1 6oz pkg instant chocolate pudding mix
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 4 eggs
* 1 cup vegetable oil
* 3/4 cup water
* 1/2 cup Bailey's Irish Cream

!!! Directions

# Preheat oven to 350°
# Grease and flour bundt pan
# Blend ingredients in bowl on low speed
# Increase speed and mix 5 minutes more
# Bake 60-70 minutes
# Let cool in pan for 10 minutes. Invert onto plate


!! Glaze
!!! Ingredients

* 1/2 cup powdered sugar
* 1/4 cup Bailey's Irish Creem

!!! Directions

# Whisk powdered sugar into Bailey's until disolved

# Punch holes in top of cake with a fork or toothpick ''[=[or a fine knitting needle!]=]'' while still warm and pour on glaze.
