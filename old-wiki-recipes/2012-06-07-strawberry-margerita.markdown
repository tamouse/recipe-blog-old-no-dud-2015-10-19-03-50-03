[[#ingredients]]
!!Ingredients
* 1 cup strawberries, hulled
* 6 tablespoons tequila
* 2 tablespoons triple sec
* 1/4 cup limeade concentrate
* 2 cups crushed ice

[[#directions]]
!!Directions

# Combine all ingredients in blender

# Process on high until frothy

# Pour into margerita glasses
