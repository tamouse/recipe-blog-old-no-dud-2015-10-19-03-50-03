This is a recipe my Mom found ages and ages ago and inflicted on us. We actually liked it.

[[#ingredients]]
!!Ingredients
* 1 pound lean ground beef
* 1 medium onion, chopped
* 1 cup chopped celery
* 1/2 head cabbage, chopped
* 1 can condensed tomato soup
* salt and pepper to taste

[[#directions]]
!!Directions
# brown ground beef, onion and celery. drain fat as it accumulates.
# in a 1.5 quart casserole, alternate layers of ground beef mixture with chopped cabbage
# pour undiluted tomato soup over top
# cover and bake at 350°F for about 1 hour

!!Comments

Makes 4 servings.
