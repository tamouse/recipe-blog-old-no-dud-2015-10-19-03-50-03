This is one of those US midwest belly pleasers. Nothing good for you here, just great noms.

!!Ingredients

* 2 tablespoon butter
* 2 eggs
* 2 cups milk
* salt and pepper to taste
* 2 cups grated yellow cheddar cheese
* 2 cups grated white cheddar cheese
* 1 cup diced ham
* 2 pound bag of tator tots, thawed


!!Directions
# Heat oven to 350°F
# Grease 9x9 dish with butter
# In bowl, whisk eggs and milk, add salt and pepper
# Add 1 cup each of the cheeses, ham and tator tots
# Mix thoroughly and carefully
# Pour into dish and sprinkle with remaining cheeses
# Bake 20-30 minutes
# Nom
