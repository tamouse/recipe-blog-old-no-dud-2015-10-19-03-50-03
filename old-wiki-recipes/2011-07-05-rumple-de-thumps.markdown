This came from a friend, Kalihva, who introduced a few different UK traditional recipes.

!!Ingredients
* 1 Rutabaga, peeled and cubed
* 2 large potatoes, peeled and cubed
* 1 cup shredded cabbage
* 1/2 stick butter
* salt and pepper
* 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

!!Directions
# Heat oven to 350°F
# Peel, cube and boil rutabaga and potatoes in pot of salted water for 20 minutes
# Sauté shredded cabbage in a little butter or olive oil until slightly wilted
# Add some butter (about 1/2 a stick should do) to drained rutabagas and potatoes and mash
# Stir in cooked cabbage
# Salt and pepper to taste
# Put mixture in baking pan and top with cheese
# Bake 30-35 minutes
